## I'm Not Patient

When researching published recipes for clementine cake, there were two things that stood out to me. 

\#1: The clementines had to be boiled whole for at least 2 hours on the stove in order for them to be soft enough to mash. 

\#2 The cake is traditionally made with gluten-free with only almond flour. However, I did find a couple recipes that used only all-purpose flour.

I used this *bakesby Brown Sugar* recipe as my base guide to build off of with several changes. Although they were one of the only recipes that didn't boil their clementines, I knew that I needed to soften mine somehow. I only had a weak blender to use to blitz them that would not be able to handle raw whole clementines. Butttt I'm lazy and didn't want to wait 2 hours for them to be ready.
