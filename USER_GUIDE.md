# Blog User Guide for Content Creators

This guide is specifically for the person who will be writing and managing blog posts. Everything here is written in simple, non-technical terms.

## üéØ Quick Start

### Your Admin Panel

Your blog's control panel is at: `https://your-site.netlify.app/admin/`

**Bookmark this page!** This is where you'll log in to write posts.

### Logging In

1. Go to your admin URL (given to you after setup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Log in"

**Forgot password?** Click "Forgot password" and follow the email instructions.

## ‚úçÔ∏è Creating Your First Post

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Log in to your admin panel**

2. **Click "Blog Posts"** in the left sidebar
   - This shows all your existing posts

3. **Click the "New Blog Posts" button** at the top

4. **Fill out your post:**

   **Title** (Required)
   - Your post's headline
   - Example: "A Weekend in Barcelona"

   **Publish Date** (Required)
   - Click the calendar icon
   - Choose the date and time
   - Posts will appear on your site based on this date

   **Featured Image** (Required)
   - This is the main image for your post
   - Click "Choose an image"
   - Either drag a file or click "Upload"
   - Wait for the upload to finish (you'll see a checkmark)

   **Excerpt** (Required)
   - A short 2-3 sentence summary
   - This appears on your homepage and in previews
   - Make it enticing!

   **Category** (Required)
   - Choose: Travel, Baking, or Lifestyle
   - This helps readers find related posts

   **Featured Post** (Optional)
   - Toggle this ON if you want the post to appear prominently on your homepage
   - Recommended for your best or most recent posts

   **Body** (Required)
   - This is your main post content
   - Write as much as you want!
   - See formatting tips below

   **Gallery Images** (Optional)
   - Add multiple photos to create a gallery
   - Click "Add gallery images"
   - Upload each image one at a time
   - Great for travel photos or step-by-step recipes

   **Tags** (Optional)
   - Keywords for your post
   - Example: "italy", "travel-tips", "budget-travel"
   - Click "Add tags" and type each tag, pressing Enter after each

5. **Save or Publish:**
   - **Save as Draft**: Click "Save" to work on it later (not published)
   - **Publish**: Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now" to make it live

## üé® Formatting Your Post Content

### Basic Formatting

**Make text bold:**
- Highlight text and click the **B** button

**Make text italic:**
- Highlight text and click the *I* button

**Create headings:**
- Click where you want the heading
- Use the dropdown (usually says "Paragraph")
- Choose "Heading 2" for main sections or "Heading 3" for subsections

**Create a list:**
- Click the bullet list button for a bulleted list
- Click the number list button for a numbered list
- Type each item and press Enter for a new item

**Add a link:**
- Highlight the text you want to link
- Click the link button (looks like a chain)
- Paste the URL
- Click "Add"

**Add a quote:**
- Highlight the text
- Click the quote button
- Great for highlighting important information

### Adding Images in Your Post

1. Place your cursor where you want the image
2. Click the image button
3. Upload your image
4. It will appear in your post

**Tip**: Images automatically look great - they're responsive and have nice styling!

## üì∏ Image Tips

### Choosing Great Images

- **Featured Image**: Use a landscape (horizontal) photo, at least 1200px wide
- **Gallery Images**: Try to keep them similar sizes
- **In-Post Images**: Any size works!

### Where to Get Images

- Your own photos (best option!)
- Free stock photos: [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com), [Pexels](https://pexels.com)
- Always credit if required by the photographer

### Before Uploading

Large files slow down your site. Compress them first:
1. Go to [tinypng.com](https://tinypng.com)
2. Upload your image
3. Download the compressed version
4. Use that for your blog

**Good rule**: Keep images under 500KB when possible

## üìù Writing Workflow

### Recommended Process

1. **Write in drafts first:**
   - Click "Save" instead of "Publish"
   - Come back later to edit
   - Click "Publish" when ready

2. **Preview before publishing:**
   - Click the eye icon to preview
   - Check formatting and images
   - Make sure everything looks good

3. **Publish:**
   - Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now"
   - Wait 1-2 minutes
   - Check your live site

### How Long Does Publishing Take?

After clicking "Publish now":
- Wait 1-2 minutes
- Your site rebuilds automatically
- Refresh your homepage to see the new post

**Don't publish multiple posts at once** - wait for each one to finish building before starting the next.

## ‚úèÔ∏è Editing Existing Posts

1. **Go to "Blog Posts"** in the sidebar
2. **Find your post** in the list
3. **Click on it** to open
4. **Make your changes**
5. **Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now"** to save
6. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for changes to appear

## üóë Deleting Posts

1. Open the post you want to delete
2. Click "Delete entry" at the top (red button)
3. Confirm you want to delete
4. The post will be removed after the site rebuilds

**Note**: Deleted posts cannot be recovered!

## ‚öôÔ∏è Changing Site Settings

### Updating Your Blog Info

1. **Click "Site Settings"** in the left sidebar
2. **Edit any field:**
   - **Site Title**: Your blog name
   - **Site Description**: What your blog is about
   - **Author Name**: Your name
   - **About Text**: Your bio or about section
   - **Social Media**: Instagram handle, Pinterest, email

3. **Click "Publish" ‚Üí "Publish now"**
4. **Wait for rebuild**

### Adding Social Media

Just enter your username (not the full URL):
- Instagram: Just "yourhandle" not "instagram.com/yourhandle"
- Pinterest: Just "yourname"
- Email: Full email address is fine

## üéØ Pro Tips for Great Posts

### Writing

- **Start strong**: Hook readers in the first paragraph
- **Use headings**: Break up text with H2 and H3 headings
- **Short paragraphs**: 2-4 sentences each for easier reading
- **Tell stories**: Personal anecdotes make posts memorable
- **Include details**: Specific details are more interesting than generic descriptions

### Images

- **More is better**: Use multiple images throughout
- **Vary the shots**: Mix wide, close-up, and detail photos
- **Show yourself**: People connect with faces!
- **Caption important images**: Use the text around images to provide context

### Categories and Tags

- **Categories**: Choose one per post
- **Tags**: Use 3-5 relevant tags
- **Be consistent**: Use the same tags across related posts

### Featured Posts

- **Keep it fresh**: Update featured posts regularly
- **Showcase your best**: Feature posts you're most proud of
- **Seasonal**: Feature timely content (holiday recipes, summer travel, etc.)

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Can I schedule posts for later?

Not automatically, but you can:
1. Save as a draft
2. Set the publish date to the future
3. Come back on that day and click "Publish"

### What happens if I lose my work?

- Click "Save" regularly while writing
- If you close the browser, your draft should be saved
- If something goes wrong, check your drafts

### Can I have multiple authors?

Yes! The person who set up the blog can invite additional users through Netlify.

### Can I change categories?

Yes, but it requires editing a configuration file. Ask the person who set up the blog to add new categories.

### Will old URLs still work if I edit a post?

Yes! Editing a post doesn't change its URL (which is based on the publish date and original title).

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?

- Check your email and password are correct
- Try the "Forgot password" link
- Make sure you accepted the invitation email
- Clear your browser cookies and try again

### Changes not showing up?

- Wait 2-3 minutes after publishing
- Hard refresh your browser (hold Shift and click reload)
- Check that you clicked "Publish now" not just "Save"

### Image not uploading?

- Check your internet connection
- Try a smaller file size
- Make sure it's a JPG or PNG file
- Try a different browser

### Getting an error message?

- Screenshot the error
- Try logging out and back in
- Contact the person who set up the blog
